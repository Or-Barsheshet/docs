Semantic Search in Visual Layer allows you to retrieve images and objects based on the meaning and context of your textual queries, rather than relying solely on exact keyword matches. This search enables more intuitive and relevant search results by understanding the concepts behind your search terms.

### Example Queries

- **Simple Query:** "sunset over mountains"
- **Detailed Query:** "bright sunset over rocky mountains with clear sky"
- **Combined Attributes:** "blue sports car on city street at night"
